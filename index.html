Mental Health Advocate:
Sgt Redmond Gary

He goes by Red and in addition to patrolling the streets of Lafayette, Red holds an Associates Degree from LSU in Behavioral Health and has a knack for listening. Red was born and raised in Lafayette and is dedicated to not only keep his community to safe but to look for opportunities to engage with those struggling with their Mental Health to help empower them for a brighter future.

Mental Health Advocate:
Penny Allstrom

Penny holds a Bachelors Degree in Social Sciences from LSU and is working toward a Masters of Social Work while serving the community of Lafayette to promote the accessiblity of resources to those in underserved areas of the City. Penny's approach to help is to listen first as she feels the best pathway for a solution is rooted in understanding.

Mental Health Advocate:
Alex Martinez

Deacon Alex Martinez holds a Graduate Degree in Thelogical Studies and currently serves as lead Deacon at the Christ Church of Lafayette. Recently returning from a mission in San Salvador, Alex believes Christ's most important work is on the streets to help those suffering from Mental Distress to find someone to help understand their situation to discover what pathways may best serve their needs.

Pathways Curator:
Emily Davis

Emily works for the Lafayette Health and Human Service department and holds a Masters Degree from LSU in Behavioral Studies. Emily's mission as the curator for this Pathways community is to help find those in the community asking for help and those willing to provide help then to align community resources with these parties to be found here.
