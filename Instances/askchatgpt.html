ASK ChatGPT

Generative AI is a tool. It should be used as such. My thoughts are that if we use generative AI as a wizard we are going to get warlocks so be mindful what you ask and as with anything in life double check the answer.

This portion of the framework will evolve to become more of an integrated feature but for right now it will remain a standalone.

Whether you are looking to help or need help, what questions could you ask to help you solidify your pathways for mental health?

Visit this link to ask ChatGPT. It is free and it does not require a login.

Here are a few starter questions!

TO ASK FOR HELP
1. “What are some grounding techniques or practices that I can use right now to calm myself?”
2. “What are some ways to reframe my negative thoughts in this moment?”
3. “Can you suggest coping strategies or short-term actions to take for immediate relief?”
4. “What are signs that I should seek immediate professional support or reach out to a crisis hotline?”
5. “What are some questions I can ask myself to better understand the root of my current feelings?”
6. “What are some mental health hotlines or text services I can reach out to for immediate support?”
7. “How can I find a local mental health professional or therapist?.
8. “Are there any online support communities or forums for people going through similar issues?”
9. “What are some apps or tools that can help me manage my mental health?”
10. “Where can I access affordable or free mental health resources if cost is a concern?”

TO ASK TO HELP
1. “How can I recognize signs that someone is in crisis and may need immediate help?”
2. “What are the best ways to approach someone who seems to be in a mental health crisis?”
3. “What language should I use or avoid to prevent triggering or escalating the situation?”
4. “What are some immediate grounding techniques I can suggest or help guide them through?”
5. “How can I encourage them to access professional help if they are hesitant?”
6. “What are some resources or hotlines I can share with them?”
7. “How can I take care of myself while supporting someone in crisis?”
8. “What should I do if the person expresses thoughts of self-harm or suicide?”
9. “How can I check in on them after the crisis without being intrusive?”
10. “What are some common myths about mental health crises that I should be aware of?”
